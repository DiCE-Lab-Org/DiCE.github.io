Data‑informed Construction Engineering

We advance Mechanical & Civil Engineering with data‑driven, technology‑based solutions for the AEC/FM industry — focusing on automation, visualization, robotics, and analytics to improve the design, construction, and management of civil infrastructure.

Research Areas

Data analytics · Automation & Robotics · Simulation & Digital Twins · Sensing & Visualization

Data Analytics & ML

Wearables, MEMS/EMG, and machine learning for safety, health, and productivity improvement in construction.

Automation & Robotics

UAVs/drones and robots for sustainability, resiliency, and energy efficiency of the built environment.

Simulation & Digital Twins

Integrating BIM, sensor data, and stochastic simulation to support real‑time decision‑making.

Sensing & Perception

Computer vision and multimodal sensing (cameras, LiDAR) for as‑built modeling and progress monitoring.

Visualization & UX

AR/VR and dashboards to make complex infrastructure data intuitive and actionable for practitioners.

Sustainability & Resilience

Tools that reduce environmental impact and increase resilience to natural hazards across the asset lifecycle.

Featured Projects

Examples of ongoing directions and pilots.

SiteVision: Real‑time Progress Monitoring

Automated tracking of quantities and safety metrics using multimodal perception and 4D BIM.

InfraTwin: Digital Twins for Bridges

Sensor‑integrated models for inspection scheduling, maintenance, and resilience planning.
